TheTrainer

Bootstrap

The Trainer is my first major project that was started toward the beginning my of Bootstrap course. This website and application is intended for personal trainers and their clients to connect.

Trainers have the capability to create custom exercises and workouts to share with their clients. These exercises will have videos and images that will show the client how they are done with proper form. The client will also be able to read a short description of the exercises and log their set, reps, and weights used. Once a workout is completed, the trainer will be notified so they can congratulate and continue to encourage their clients. I created a temporary chat component, which will be built in the near future for continued communication between the personal trainer and their clients.

I believe my biggest challenges with this project were to implement my ideas to a wire fire and to actual code, plus creating the login component. With some minor edits to the JQuery, the user can select 'Client' or 'Trainer' and only have access to certain things.
