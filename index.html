Who I am

Designer & Developer based out of HTX

My name is Raul and I am an aspiring web and mobile developer. My journey began during the summer of 2021 when I was curious about the coding and the tech industry. I began by looking through some instructional YouTube videos, subscribed to Mosh Hamedani's program, and finally signed up for Nucamp's Introduction to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Once I began, I knew this was going to be a special journey for me and my family; therefore, I later enrolled in their Full Stack Web and Mobile Development Bootcamp.

During the Spring of 2022, I hit pause on Software Development for 2 years as my family and I moved to New Mexico to continue my career in Education. We loved the mountains, outdoor activities, and the dryer climate in New Mexico! Our time in New Mexico was cut short and our lives brought back closer to family in Houston. This is when I decided to continue my education in Software Engineering. I began the Software Engineering program through SMU/Flatiron School in November 2024 and will complete it in August 2025.

I have been married to my beautiful wife Kaitlin for 12 years and we have 3 amazing children. I have been band director for 12 years and have loved the teaching aspect, but have also found my niche in design and creativity. Through my years of teaching, and the connections and relationships I've made in the music world, I've found my true passion of software development.
