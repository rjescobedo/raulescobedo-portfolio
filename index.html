Who I am

Designer & Developer based out of HTX

I’m Raul, a web developer and designer based in Houston, TX. As the founder of Raul’s Web Studio, I specialize in crafting custom websites for small businesses, entrepreneurs, and creatives who want to stand out online. With a background in education and a passion for technology, I bring a unique blend of problem-solving, creativity, and attention to detail to every project I take on.

My journey into web development began in 2021, sparked by a curiosity for coding and a drive to build meaningful digital experiences. I started with online courses and hands-on projects before enrolling in Nucamp’s Full Stack Web and Mobile Development Bootcamp, where I gained a solid foundation in front-end and back-end technologies. Since then, I’ve continued sharpening my skills through the Software Engineering program at SMU/Flatiron School and Scrimba’s Front-End Developer Career Path.

Before launching Raul’s Web Studio, I spent 12 years as a band director, where I developed leadership, project management, and creative problem-solving skills—qualities that now shape my approach to web development. Today, I’m passionate about helping businesses establish their online presence with functional, beautifully designed websites that drive results.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me spending time with my wife and three kids, exploring design trends, or going to a Houston Astros and Dynamo game. If you’re looking for a website that not only looks great but also works seamlessly, let’s chat and bring your vision to life!
