Co-Ed Softball Life Group

HTML/CSS/JavaScript/Bootstrap

Co-Ed Softball Life Group is a website designed to bring people together through faith, fellowship, and sports. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, the site serves as a central hub for a faith-based co-ed softball group, making it easy for members to stay connected, view weekly lessons, and engage with their community.

Using Bootstrap, I created a clean, responsive design that ensures a seamless experience across all devices. The site is structured to be both visually appealing and highly functional, providing easy navigation and quick access to important information.

This project showcases my ability to develop user-friendly, dynamic websites that enhance group interactions. It reflects my commitment to building digital spaces that foster connection and accessibility, whether on the field or online.
