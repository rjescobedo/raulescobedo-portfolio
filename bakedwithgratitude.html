Baked withGratitude

Baked with Gratitude is a custom website designed to help a small baking business showcase its products and connect with customers. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the site prioritizes simplicity, elegance, and functionality, offering a seamless user experience across both desktop and mobile devices. Key features include intuitive navigation, product galleries, and responsive design elements that enhance accessibility and engagement.

This project exemplifies my expertise in front-end web development and my ability to craft tailored digital solutions for small businesses. Interactive elements, such as hover effects and streamlined forms, were integrated to enhance customer interaction and usability. The design reflects the brand’s warmth and quality, demonstrating how a well-executed website can strengthen a business’s online presence and support its growth.
