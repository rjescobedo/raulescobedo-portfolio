TheTrainer

React

The Trainer is my first major project, initiated during the early stages of my Bootstrap course. This website and application are designed to help personal trainers and their clients connect seamlessly.

Trainers can create and share custom exercises and workouts tailored to their clients' needs. Each exercise includes instructional videos and images to demonstrate proper form, along with a brief description for guidance. Clients can log their sets, reps, and weights directly within the app. Once a workout is completed, trainers are notified, allowing them to celebrate their clientsâ€™ progress and provide continued motivation. In the near future, I plan to incorporate a chat feature to facilitate ongoing communication about training sessions, custom workouts, and more.

One of the biggest challenges I faced was transitioning this project from basic HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to a React application. Along the way, I refined the layout and functionality to ensure an intuitive and enjoyable user experience. Looking ahead, I plan to convert this project to React Native, making it accessible as both a website and a mobile app. My ultimate goal is to offer this solution to gyms and personal trainers, empowering them to elevate their services.
