Lil' SqueezersLemonade Stand

Lil' Squeezers is a family-run lemonade stand operated by my kids, bringing homemade, refreshing lemonade to our local community. To support their small business, I developed a dedicated website that serves as a central hub for stand updates, schedules, and digital payment options, streamlining the customer experience.

Built in March 2025, this website was designed to provide a simple yet engaging way for my kids to manage their business efficiently. It features real-time updates on locations and operating hours, along with seamless integration of Venmo and Zelle for convenient cashless transactions. With a clean, mobile-friendly design, the site ensures accessibility for customers on the go. This project not only highlights my expertise in front-end development and digital payment solutions but also reflects my passion for creating functional, user-friendly websites that empower small businesses—whether run by young entrepreneurs or established professionals.
